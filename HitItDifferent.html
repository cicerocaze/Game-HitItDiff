HitItDiff

HitItDiff is a simple casual game which aims to maintain a ball in the squared white area, using two bars, controling them with both hands (green is left hand, orange is right hand). Every time the ball hit on the wall (brown bar), the gamer receives a point. The point is given only if the last rebut is made with a different bar (hand).

Controls:

GREEN BAR (Left Hand) - Use "A" to UP and "Z" to DOWN.

ORANGE BAR (Right Hand) - Use "K" to UP and "M" to DOWN.
